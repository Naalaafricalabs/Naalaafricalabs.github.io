Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How long does it take to develop a custom AI solution?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most custom AI solutions take 8-16 weeks from discovery to deployment. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do I need to have data ready before starting?

Not necessarily. During our discovery phase, we assess your data landscape and help you identify, collect, and prepare the data needed. We can also work with limited data using techniques like transfer learning and data augmentation.

What kind of ROI can I expect from AI solutions?

ROI varies by use case, but our clients typically see 30-50% efficiency improvements, 20-40% cost reductions, or significant revenue increases within 6-12 months. We provide ROI projections during the planning phase.

Do you provide training for our team?

Yes! We provide comprehensive training for your team as part of every project. We also offer standalone AI training workshops and courses to upskill your entire organization.

What ongoing support do you provide?

We offer flexible support packages including monitoring, maintenance, updates, and optimization. Our team is available to ensure your AI solution continues to deliver value as your business grows.

How much does a custom AI solution cost?

Costs vary based on project scope, complexity, and requirements. Projects typically range from $10,000 to $100,000+. We offer flexible payment plans and provide detailed quotes after the free consultation.
